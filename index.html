Insomnia: the Sleepless Week

1 week.
3 participants in team.
6 teams in cars.
How long can they stay awake?

Qualification: April 2019, Central Latvia
Baltic race: June 2019
Baltics Scandinavian race: June 2020, Scandinavia
A week filled with adrenaline, adventures, fun, rush and best of all - free of charge for the sake of cash prize. 
Can it get any better?

Well, yes, there is a catch. There always is.

All this comes for the price of sleep.
In the rules time for sleep is not reserved.

Sleep management is the key to victory. Because of exhaustion participants will become their own worst enemies. We know it.

If you dare to consider taking part in this adventure of a lifetime, come follow us in social networks to be among the first ones to get details!
