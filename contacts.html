About Us

The race emerged in Riga, the capital of Latvia, in a historic region known as the Republic of Crows street.
On January 1st, 2016, 7 adventure-addicted mad guys came together following a post in a Facebook group and for the first time heard the idea developed by Edgars.
During the painful process of forming the idea, the mighty seven first turned into hateful eight, later to become the magnificent ten.
Since the day the idea was spoken out loud for the first time, the minds of the magnificent ten have shaped the race to perfection you see today.
