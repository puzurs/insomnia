The Baltics Race

At the end of June 2019 in the southern Lithuania, the Baltics race of Insomnia: the Sleepless Week takes of. At start each of six teams has their car, a smartphone with the app installed, a bunch of equipment and a task. Having completed the task, the team receives next one. 

Task by task, and during a very intense week, each team crosses all three Baltic states to finish in northern Estonia. The race takes teams to some of the most popular tourist places in all three Baltic states. Still, that doesn`t mean the tasks are easy.

Kinds of the race tasks include but not limited to hiking in national parks, trespassing in Soviet military bases, doing some snowboarding, finding out what "nerija" means, having fun in artistic districts of some cities, finding crosses in "eerie" mountains, paddling in rivers, getting over waterfalls, cycling down some country roads, scaling some sea crossings and visiting some sea islands, entertaining in children playgrounds, exploring various underground and underwater structures both active and abandoned, and a lot more.
