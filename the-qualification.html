The Qualification

Qualification takes place at the end of April, two months before the race itself in central Latvia. Each participant competes for himself.

Ideally, we`d like to receive applications from 50 men and 50 women. Out of them, nine best men and nine best women are raffled in six teams with three participants of both sexes in each team. The raffle takes place after the qualification as soon as the qualification results are known. 

Qualification is 24 hours control time starting Saturday midday. It contains four stages: cycling, orienteering, trail running and paddling. However, all stages include surprises. We worked really hard on them and we believe you know what that means. Some are so sick that even we are scared of them. 

Participants have to walk, run, paddle, dive, bicycle, shoot, orientate, rope-climb, bungee-jump, slack-line, do some track and field athletics, solve various tasks and a lot more.

Although in the race all expenses for participants are covered, in the qualification there`s an application fee. It`s quite high. Yes, we know it and we have a reason for it.
