Participants

Participant must be between 18 and 65 years old and hold a valid driver`s licence. Before the start, all participants have to sign a contract which confirms that each participant takes full responsibility for himself in the race. 

Our ideal participant in not only physically strong, tough and durable but also smart, stress-resistant and able to make the right strategic decisions under heavy pressure. Ability to work in a team and to respect each other's strengths and weaknesses is very important. 

Having knowledge of action sports and other adrenaline-filled activities is also a major advantage. So – if you dare and meet the requirements, follow us to be among the first ones to know when the application for the race is open. 

Application fee also includes a pledge for the participant to be ready to take part in the race itself two months later in case he or she is among the 18 best ones. Pledge will be returned to the participant after the race unless he or she qualifies for the race but is unable to participate. 

There`s only application fee to lose but there`s adventure of a lifetime to win.
